Top Three Places to Visit This Year

  1. Banff Canada
  2. Cannon Beach, Oregon
    • The best time of year being between the months of May and September
  3. La Plata, Argentina
    • The best time of the year being between the months of September and November









Visit beautiful Haystack rock, the tide pools, lay on the beach, or go on a stroll for some amazing cuisine here at Cannon Beach.










Gorgeous Banff is the perfect spot to find serenity surrounded by beautiful lakes and the Canadian Wilderness.











There is so much to do in the beautiful Capitial City of La plata, Buenos Aires, Argentina. Don't miss out on the amazing history at the cathedral in the middle of the city!
